Briefs about Location

Around Hotel Greenland Delsol

Get away from the maddening pollution of Mumbai, the hustle and bustle of the insanely active city and come, enjoy the quiet greens of Lonavala and Khandala It is your window to a breathtaking panoramic view of the valley. And during the monsoon you can look down upon the dark, mysterious clouds rumbling and tumbling, with the streaking waterfall as its silver lining, as if quietly descending from the heavens so as not to disturb the peace and harmony of nature.

So pack your bags and take the first train to Khandala. Leave your worries aside. There is that top of the world feeling waiting for you ... at Lonavala and khandala.

Best Location for Conference

Whether you are planning a workshop, a management seminar, marketing or training session, these hotels can customise their facilities to meet your individual needs and ensure a memorable event. They are also glad to help organise special theme evenings and group activities. Meeting rooms at Lonavala and Khandala Hotels & Resorts are fully equipped with state-of-the-art communications systems and presentation facilities to ensure the smooth running of your business event

AIR: the nearest Airport from Lonavala & Khandala is Lohgaon (Pune) 82 km.

RAIL: Lonavala and Khandala are Mumbai - Pune main line of the Central Railway. All the trains running between Mumbai and Pune and beyond, halt at Lonavala.

LOCAL TRANSPORT: Unmetered taxies and Auto-rickshaws are available at Lonavala Railway Station, S.T.Stand and Bazar. The State Road Transport buses starting from Lonavala S.T. Stand are available for visiting places of tourist interest like Karla Caves and Rajmachi Piont.

LOCATION

How To Reach Hotel Greenland Delsol

Take the Khandala Exit and get onto Old Mumbai - Pune Highway, take the lane next to L&T Management Centre and head straight and after crossing a small bridge on the left side is Hotel Greenland Delsol.

  • Pune To (Lonavala) By Car via Mumbai-Pune Expressway :

    • Travel Duration : 1.25 Hours
    Road Distance : 90 Kms
    Take the Sion-Panvel Highway.Head straight towards onto Mumbai-Pune Expressway.Take khandala exit move towards National Highway 4 which will lead to Lonavala Main Market/Kumar Resort.

  • Pune To (Lonavala) By Car via Mumbai-Pune Expressway

    • Travel Duration : 1.25 Hours
    Road Distance : 70 Kms
    Head to Mumbai-Pune Expressway via Baner/Aundh/Kothrud. Take the Lonavala Exit and head straight wherein you will reach the Lonavala Market.

  • Pune To (Lonavala) By Train

    • Travel Duration : 1 Hours
    Get down at Lonavala Railway Station there is a road which will lead you to Lonavala ST Stand and further ahead on that road is Lonavala Main Market.

  • Pune To (Lonavala) By Bus via National Highway 4/NH 4

    • Travel Duration : 1 Hours
    Board any State transport bus from Shivajinagar/Swargate which will lead to Lonavala ST Stand whereas if taken a Neeta volvo, it will drop you at Neeta Volvo stop which is near the Exit of Lonavala from where frequent Six-seaters rickshaws are available.

  • Mumbai To (Lonavala) By Train

    • Travel Duration : 2.5 Hours
    Get down at Lonavala Railway Station there is a footpath which will lead you to Lonavala ST Stand and further ahead on that road is Lonavala Main Market.

  • Mumbai To (Lonavala) By Bus via National Highway 4/NH 4

    • Travel Duration : 3 Hours
    Board any State transport bus from Mumbai(Dadar/Borivali/Mumbai Central),which will lead to Lonavala ST Stand whereas if taken a Neeta Volvo, it will drop you at Khandala Exit(on Highway) & from there auto-rickshaws are available

  • Mumbai To (Lonavala) By Car via Mumbai-Pune Expressway

    Travel Duration : 2.5 Hours
    Road Distance : 90 Kms
    Take the Sion-Panvel Highway.Head straight towards onto Mumbai-Pune Expressway.Take khandala exit move towards National Highway 4 which will lead to Lonavala Main Market/Kumar Resort.
